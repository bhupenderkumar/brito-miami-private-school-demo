Our Mission & History

Our school's history dates back to 2005, when Dr. Antonio Brito founded Brito Miami Private School with the vision of creating a world-class educational institution in the heart of Miami. Today, we continue to grow and evolve, always staying true to our core values of academic rigor, creativity, and community service.

At Brito Miami Private School, we believe in a holistic approach to education that goes beyond the classroom. Our dedicated teachers and staff work tirelessly to create engaging, hands-on learning experiences that challenge students to think critically and creatively.

From sports teams to art clubs, our extracurricular programs are designed to help students develop valuable skills, build lasting friendships, and discover new passions. Whether it's debate team or band, we believe that every student has something unique to offer.
