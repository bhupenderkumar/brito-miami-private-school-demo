Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum that challenges, inspires, and prepares every student.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-Kindergarten program is designed for children aged 3-4 years old. We provide a nurturing and engaging environment that fosters social, emotional, and cognitive development.

📚

Elementary

Our Elementary program serves students from Kindergarten to 5th grade. We offer a comprehensive curriculum that includes language arts, mathematics, science, and social studies, as well as electives such as music, art, and physical education.

🤓

Middle/Upper

Our Middle/Upper program serves students from 6th to 8th grade. We continue to build on the foundation established in Elementary, offering advanced courses in mathematics, science, and language arts, as well as electives in foreign languages, technology, and the arts.
