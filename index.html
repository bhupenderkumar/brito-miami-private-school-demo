Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum that challenges, inspires, and prepares every student.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-K program is designed for young learners aged 3-4, providing a nurturing environment that fosters social, emotional, and cognitive growth. Our experienced teachers use play-based learning to introduce essential skills like literacy, numeracy, and creativity.

📚

Elementary

In our elementary program, students in grades K-4 discover the joy of learning through hands-on activities, project-based learning, and small class sizes. Our dedicated teachers provide individualized support to ensure each student reaches their full potential.

📊

Middle/Upper

Our middle and upper school program serves students in grades 5-8, offering a rigorous academic curriculum that prepares them for success in high school and beyond. Our experienced teachers provide guidance and support to help students navigate this critical phase of their educational journey.
